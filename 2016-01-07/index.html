Overview of Networks

What is a network?

  • End Systems, e.g., computers, phones, servers.
  • Communication Links, e.g., cell broadcasts, broadband
  • Intermediate Systems, e.g., routers, switches

Types of Networks

  • Broadcast
    • Cable TV
  • Switched
    • Circuit Switching
      • Public Switched Telephone Network (PSTN)
    • Packet Switching
      • The Internet

Packet Switching

Packets must arrive to the router before it is forwarded to its next destination. This is called store and forward.

Example

You have a packet that is 7.5 Mbits which goes through 3 routers and each router processes at 1.5 Mbps.
